Canvas Print (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you use our website or request our services, and how we handle it.

Information we collect

  • Contact details you provide (name, email, phone, company) when submitting a quote or message.
  • Project details and any files you upload for printing.
  • Basic technical data such as browser type and pages visited.

How we use your information

  • To prepare quotations and fulfil your print orders.
  • To communicate with you about your enquiry or order.
  • To improve our website and services.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share it with trusted partners (such as courier services) where necessary to deliver your order.

Data retention

We keep your information only as long as needed to provide our services and meet legal obligations, after which it is securely deleted.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at hello@canvasprintbd.com.
